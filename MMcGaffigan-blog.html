My Musical Journey

Welcome to All About Mike! As you know by now, I've spent the last few years of my life being a traveling musician, playing drums in a band. I've never gone into detail about how I got started, or what I did to get that opportunity. Today that changes! This week's post will be about how I got started, and where I'm at now.

How it started

Ever since I was a child, I've been very drawn to music. I recognized that all music had a pattern and it fascinated me. My parents signed me up for piano lessons when I was nine to further nurture my interest. After time, I grew bored of piano, as I got into heavier, louder music. I began playing drums when I was 13, and guitar when I was 15. I became immediatley fixated on both instruments and began playing guitar in a band when I was 18. After playing guitar in a local punk band for a few months, I decided to switch over to drums and played in a grunge band from ages 18-22. Upon moving to Marquette, Michigan at age 22, I joined a different punk band who wanted to take their act on the road.

Where I'm at now

After leaving the previously mentioned band in April of this year, I took some time to myself to reflect on where I wanted to go with the music. Currently, I'm putting more time into writing and recording my own music, as well as drumming in a local Folk-Rock band and Serving as a fill-in drummer for the very popular, Michigan-based act, Liquid Mike! Overall, I'm thankful for the ups and downs, and everything in between that has made this journey what it is.

It's never wrong to follow your passion! I believe following your passion is the key to a happy life.
